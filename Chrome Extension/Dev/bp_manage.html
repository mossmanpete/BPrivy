Sync Wallets

To enable internet-based sync, just create or move your Wallet folder into a Dropbox, Google Drive, Microsoft Skydrive or any other such cloud-drive. However, for added security be sure to remove the Key-file from the Wallet and store it on a local drive or a thumb drive or memory card. IT IS NOT SAFE TO KEEP YOUR PASSWORDS ON THE INTERNET. However, if you must resort to cloud-based sync, we recommend that you store the Key-file in a different location than the cloud-drive.

Note: Privy Wallet™ never transfers your passwords to anywhere other than the file-system folder - of your choosing - and - temporarily - the memory of the computer/device on which it is running. It certainly does not transfer them to any internet web-site or server. A network/internet/cloud/sky drive however, *will* transfer your passwords to locations outside of your control from where they could be lost, stolen, damaged or otherwise compromised.
